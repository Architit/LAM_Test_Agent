# MASTER PLAN: LIVE-CORRECTION V0.1

## 1. Mission
Build a resilient multi-agent control ecosystem for:
- life support
- water
- energy
- physical/material supply
- extraction
- production
- logistics
- environment
- governance/security
- digital life domains

## 2. Operating Loops
- Strategic Loop: every 24h (target reset, capacity, budget/risk limits).
- Tactical Loop: every 4h (task allocation, priority rebalance).
- Operational Loop: every 5-15 min (telemetry, compliance, corrections).
- Emergency Loop: immediate (red-line breach response).

## 3. Command Model
- Directive -> Contract Validation -> Execution -> Safety Gate -> Verification -> Audit.
- No high-impact action without contract ID.
- No persistent install/escalation without explicit operator consent.

## 4. Domains and Owners
- GOV_CTRL: policies, contracts, decision journal.
- LIFE_CTRL: life-support continuity and fallback.
- WATER_CTRL: flow/quality/loss management.
- ENERGY_CTRL: generation/storage/load shedding policy.
- RESOURCE_CTRL: inventory and minimum stock thresholds.
- EXTRACTION_CTRL: extraction plans and safety limits.
- PROD_CTRL: production plan-fact and bottleneck control.
- LOGI_CTRL: route/SLA and disruption handling.
- ENV_CTRL: environmental constraints and anomaly response.
- CYBERPHY_CTRL: cyber + physical gateway defense.
- DIGITAL_LIFE_CTRL: AI role rights, duty and ethics boundaries.

## 5. Phase Plan
### Phase A (0-72h)
- Inventory all active agents with owner and contract baseline.
- Enable unified telemetry and audit stream.
- Set red-lines and emergency escalation matrix.

### Phase B (3-14d)
- Roll out role orchestration profiles by node type.
- Implement domain-specific SLOs and queue priorities.
- Enforce preflight/security gates on all install paths.

### Phase C (2-6w)
- Capacity and redundancy expansion (multi-node, multi-provider storage).
- Formalize simulation drills for water/energy/logistics outages.
- Introduce policy auto-heal with bounded authority.

## 6. Required Artifacts
- Agent contract per runtime service.
- Directive vector map and routing matrix.
- Emergency runbooks for P1/P2/P3 incidents.
- Monthly policy review and residual-risk register.

## 7. KPIs
- P1 detection <= 30 sec; mitigation <= 60 sec.
- Contract coverage >= 95% of active agents.
- Emergency drill pass rate >= 90%.
- Unauthorized escalation events = 0.

## 8. Governance Constraints
- Contracts-First.
- Observability-First.
- Least privilege by default.
- Full auditability for security-critical operations.
